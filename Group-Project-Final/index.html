Coding in Seattle

Seattle is one of the most important cities for tech. It is the home of multiple tech giants (Microsoft, Google, etc). It is also full of multiple tech startups. This combination means that 9.9% of Seattle citizens are employed in Tech talent jobs. Further, this abundance of tech grew with the pandemic as other companies had to fire workers to conserve money and stay safe, but most tech companies had access to remote working technology, and were able to keep most of their workers. Why Java?

Java has a lot of advantages over the average programming language

  • Easy to Learn: has many tutorials and can be easily picked up
  • Universal: Functions on every OS and Platform
  • Extensive APIs: a lot of packages to do everything
  • Great Documentation: easy to understand how a method works
  • Continually Updating: Bug fixes and new features are released regularly
  • Best language for App Development, especially for Android Apps
  • Faster than python because it's a different type of language
